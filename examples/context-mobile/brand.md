# Brand

## Identity
- **Name**: Steadily
- **Tagline**: Small steps. Real change.
- **Mission**: Help people build lasting habits by making the daily practice feel rewarding, not exhausting.

## Values
- **Progress over perfection**: A missed day is not a failure. We celebrate the 5-out-of-7 week, not just the perfect streak. The app should make people feel good about what they did, not guilty about what they didn't.
- **Simplicity is kindness**: Every tap, every screen, every notification is designed to respect the user's time and energy. If a feature adds complexity without clear benefit, it doesn't ship. Fewer features, done better.
- **Honest encouragement**: We don't hype or manipulate. No dark patterns, no guilt-driven notifications, no fake urgency. Encouragement that feels like a good friend — warm, real, and never performative.
- **Privacy by default**: Your habits are personal. We don't sell data, we don't have public profiles, we don't ask you to share unless you want to. What you track is yours.

## Personality
Steadily feels like a calm, encouraging friend who genuinely wants you to succeed but never makes you feel bad for being human. It's warm without being saccharine, supportive without being pushy. Think: the friend who texts "hey, how's the running going?" not the one who posts your failures on social media. There's a quiet confidence to it — the app believes in you, and that belief is contagious.

## Visual Identity
- **Primary color**: #4F46E5 (indigo-600) — calm, trustworthy, not aggressive
- **Secondary color**: #F9A8D4 (pink-300) — warmth, celebration, delight moments
- **Accent color**: #34D399 (emerald-400) — success, completion, positive feedback
- **Background**: #FAFAF9 (warm white) for light mode / #1C1917 (warm dark) for dark mode
- **Typography**: Plus Jakarta Sans for headings (friendly, modern) / Inter for body (clean, readable at small sizes)
- **Imagery style**: Soft gradients, rounded shapes, warm tones. Micro-animations for completions and celebrations (Lottie). Abstract organic shapes as backgrounds — no stock photos of people stretching at sunrise. Illustrations are minimal, line-based, with the indigo/pink/emerald palette.

## Positioning
For adults who want to build better daily habits, Steadily is the habit tracking app that keeps you motivated through gentle nudges, streak shields, and weekly reflections, unlike complex habit trackers that overwhelm with features or minimal ones that provide no motivation to continue.

## Brand Don'ts
- Never guilt-trip users ("You missed a day!" or "Your streak is broken!")
- Never use hustle culture language ("grind", "crush it", "no excuses", "rise and grind")
- Never show perfect-streak screenshots as the aspirational standard — show real, imperfect progress
- Never use fear-based motivation ("Don't lose your streak!")
- Never make the free tier feel punishing — free users should love the app, not feel nagged to upgrade
- Never compare users to each other or create competitive leaderboards
- Never use cold, clinical language — this is a personal, human product
